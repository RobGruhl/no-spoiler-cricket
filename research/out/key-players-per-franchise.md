# key-players-per-franchise

**Chennai Super Kings**: MS Dhoni (wicketkeeper; **trademark leadership and finishing**), Ruturaj Gaikwad (batter; **trademark captaincy and opening stability**).[1]

**Royal Challengers Bengaluru**: Venkatesh Iyer (allrounder; **trademark big-hitting and pace bowling**), Virat Kohli (assumed retained batter; **trademark aggressive batting**).[4]

**Sunrisers Hyderabad**: Travis Head (batter; **trademark explosive opening**), Pat Cummins (allrounder/captain; **trademark leadership and pace**).[1]

**Mumbai Indians**: Rohit Sharma (batter; **trademark captaincy and strokeplay**), Jasprit Bumrah (fast bowler; **trademark yorkers and variations**).[1]

**Kolkata Knight Riders**: Shreyas Iyer (assumed retained batter/captain; **trademark middle-order anchoring**), Varun Chakravarthy (spinner; **trademark mystery spin**).[1]

**Lucknow Super Giants**: KL Rahul (assumed retained batter/captain; **trademark elegant opening**), Nicholas Pooran (assumed retained wicketkeeper; **trademark power-hitting**).[4]

**Delhi Capitals**: Axar Patel (allrounder; **trademark spin and fielding**), Kuldeep Yadav (spinner; **trademark left-arm wristspin**).[1]

**Rajasthan Royals**: Sanju Samson (wicketkeeper; **trademark innovative captaincy and keeping**), Yuzvendra Chahal (spinner; **trademark leg-spin guile**).[1]

**Punjab Kings**: Arshdeep Singh (fast bowler; **trademark death bowling**), Shikhar Dhawan (assumed retained batter; **trademark opening aggression**).[1]

**Gujarat Titans**: Shubman Gill (batter/captain; **trademark elegant strokeplay**), Mohammed Siraj (fast bowler; **trademark raw pace**).[1]
