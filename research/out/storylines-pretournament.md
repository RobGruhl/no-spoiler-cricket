# IPL 2026 Pre-Tournament Storylines

- **RCB's Title Defence:** Royal Challengers Bengaluru won their maiden IPL title in 2025 and enter 2026 as defending champions under new captain Rajat Patidar, with Virat Kohli remaining in the squad.[2]

- **Cameron Green's Record Transfer:** Australian all-rounder Cameron Green joined Kolkata Knight Riders for ₹25.20 crore, becoming the most expensive overseas player in IPL history.[2]

- **MS Dhoni's Potential Farewell:** Chennai Super Kings' iconic captain MS Dhoni faces what could be his final IPL season as the franchise undergoes a rebuild after finishing last in 2025.[2][3]

- **Sanju Samson's CSK Move:** After 11 of his 13 IPL seasons at Rajasthan Royals, Samson transferred to Chennai Super Kings to provide top-order firepower, with the franchise hoping to replicate his impact for the Indian national team.[3]

- **Vaibhav Suryavanshi's Breakout Year:** The teenage opener and one of IPL's youngest players had a stunning debut last season and enters 2026 with enormous expectations at Rajasthan Royals.[2]

- **CSK's Rebuild Challenge:** Chennai Super Kings aim to bounce back from a four-win, last-place finish in 2025 with a revamped squad led by Ruturaj Gaikwad.[3][4]

- **Mumbai Indians' Stacked Squad:** The Mumbai Indians entered 2026 with what observers described as an exceptionally strong squad composition.[4]
