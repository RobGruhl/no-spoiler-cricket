§ 03 — Threads to Follow

What decides this Test

India have won the last four Border-Gavaskar series at home. Australia's only win in India this century was 2004-05.
Pat Cummins' side arrives with the 2023 WTC crown and the 2025-26 Ashes in recent memory — this series measures their subcontinental adaptation.
Ashwin-era Indian spin attacks and Kuldeep/Axar rotations tend to dominate at home.
