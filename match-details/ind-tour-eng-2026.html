§ 04 — Storylines

Narratives to watch

  • India has never won a series in England since 2007 — the last tour was defining for the post-Kohli/Rohit generation.
  • Dukes ball behaviour in June–August can swing dramatically between cloudy and sunny sessions at the same venue.
  • England's Bazball era has produced high-scoring, high-stakes Tests; India's response tempo will shape every match.
§ 05 — Historical Context

Form book & lore

India's first tour of England was in 1932. The Pataudi Trophy is contested when England tour India; the Anthony de Mello Trophy when India tour England. India's only series wins in England remain 1971, 1986, and 2007.

§ 06 — Viewing Notes

When to tune in

Play typically runs 11:00–18:00 BST (15:30–22:30 IST) with a lunch and tea break. For US East Coast, mornings line up well with the first session.
